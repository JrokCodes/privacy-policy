Privacy Policy - REVMAX Voicemail System

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

1. Introduction

REVMAX Automations LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the REVMAX Voicemail System. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when healthcare providers use our voicemail transcription service.

2. Information We Process

Our service processes:

  • Voicemail audio files from healthcare providers
  • Email addresses for authentication and delivery
  • Transcribed text from voicemails

3. How We Use Information

We process information solely to:

  • Transcribe voicemail messages
  • Deliver transcriptions to authorized recipients
  • Maintain service functionality and security

4. HIPAA Compliance

We maintain HIPAA compliance through:

  • Business Associate Agreements with all healthcare clients
  • Encryption of all data in transit and at rest
  • Automatic deletion of audio files after 30 days
  • Strict access controls and audit logging

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is:

  • Stored on encrypted AWS servers in the United States
  • Protected by industry-standard security measures
  • Accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Subject to automatic deletion policies

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide our functionality:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - Infrastructure and storage
  • Deepgram - Medical transcription services
  • Google Workspace - Document creation and email delivery

All third-party services are covered by appropriate Business Associate Agreements.

7. Data Retention

  • Audio files: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Transcriptions: Retained as specified by client agreement
  • Audit logs: Retained for 7 years per HIPAA requirements

8. Your Rights

Healthcare providers and their authorized users have the right to:

  • Access their transcription data
  • Request correction of errors
  • Request deletion where permitted by law
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures

9. Google API Services

Our use of Google API Services is subject to Google's Limited Use requirements. We only access:

  • Gmail for reading voicemail messages
  • Google Drive for storing transcriptions
  • Google Docs for creating formatted documents

We do not use this data for any purpose other than providing the voicemail transcription service.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify clients of any material changes via email.
